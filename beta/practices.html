Virtual Reality - AirV

AttainX has partnered with AirV Labs Inc. to provide support for a Virtual Reality platform called AirV Platform. It consists of:

  • Authoring Tool– which allows presenter to create rich interactive 360 video, VR models and a range of other media
  • Application – Deploy VR content
    • 360 Interact – Training and Safety
    • 360 Analytics – Analysis automation for optimization
    • 360 Recall – Industrial training for step by step instructions
  • Collaboration Deployment – presentation over the net or downloadable content
  • AI Platform – saves data and provides custom content and assessment using AI

It’s a technology developed at University of Illinois at Urbana Champagne (UIUC) and licensed to AirV Labs. Its tested and validated in multiple client sites and peer reviewed in the world’s premier VR academic conference. Based on ground breaking patented mixed reality technology, AirV Labs creates training and infection control protocols for health professionals including information modules for general public. Additionally, we will dedicate our resources and use of technology to bring rapid training and prevention procedures to help with the outbreak of COVID-19. The AirV Platform includes the following:

  • Mixed Reality (MR) Technology that combines 360 Degree video with VR
  • A rapid content creation tool – reducing development time by 90%
  • Enhanced with AI tools for content and assessment automation
  • Deployment platform for teaching VR lessons on-line or off-line


Robotic Process Automation

Our team of certified UiPath Robotic Process Automation (RPA) professionals provide services using Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning capabilities for automating tasks that are mundane and require little or no human involvement. We can implement an RPA solution that meets an organization’s needs without changing its existing IT infrastructure and systems and can be seamlessly integrated with the enterprise applications.

Our team will conduct extensive analysis of an organization’s existing business processes to identify areas that can benefit from automation. Post analysis, we can rapidly deploy an RPA solution using UiPath which is cost effective and scalable with a low code design. We will provide analytical capabilities by developing reports and metrics on robots, servers, workflows etc. With an RPA solution, the organization will see tangible benefits which include:

  • Increased efficiency by focusing on more critical functions that require human intelligence and critical thinking
  • Cost reduction by replacing human potential with a machine
  • Reduction in operational risk by eliminating the need to outsource manual and repetitive tasks as the solution will be managed internally
  • Better accuracy as the solution reduces inefficiencies due to human error and the automated bots are programmed to follow rules based on the organization’s policies and procedures
  • Improved compliance as the solution can be configured to follow US Government regulations and standards
