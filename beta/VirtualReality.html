AttainX has partnered with AirV Labs Inc. to provide support for a Virtual Reality platform called AirV Platform. It consists of:

  • Authoring Tool– which allows presenter to create rich interactive 360 video, VR models and a range of other media
  • Application – Deploy VR content
    • 360 Interact – Training and Safety
    • 360 Analytics – Analysis automation for optimization
    • 360 Recall – Industrial training for step by step instructions
  • Collaboration Deployment – presentation over the net or downloadable content
  • AI Platform – saves data and provides custom content and assessment using AI

It’s a technology developed at University of Illinois at Urbana Champagne (UIUC) and licensed to AirV Labs. Its tested and validated in multiple client sites and peer reviewed in the world’s premier VR academic conference. Based on ground breaking patented mixed reality technology, AirV Labs creates training and infection control protocols for health professionals including information modules for general public. Additionally, we will dedicate our resources and use of technology to bring rapid training and prevention procedures to help with the outbreak of COVID-19. The AirV Platform includes the following:

  • Mixed Reality (MR) Technology that combines 360 Degree video with VR
  • A rapid content creation tool – reducing development time by 90%
  • Enhanced with AI tools for content and assessment automation
  • Deployment platform for teaching VR lessons on-line or off-line
