    • Prepare the writing instructions for all participants.
    • Develop the proposal schedule through submission.

    • Create proposal Master documents and manage document versions.
    • Coordinate preparation of materials for kickoff meeting and review sessions
  • Demonstration of the necessary personality and leadership skills to engage and motivate a diverse group of Engineers, Project Managers, Consultants and Subcontractors is required.
  • Outstanding communication skills (written and oral); including a strong working knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite is a necessity.
  • Exceptional facilitation and organization skills are required.
  • Ability to work a flexible schedule.

Minimum Qualifications/ Skills Set :

  • Bachelor’s degree with 3 years’ experience in government contract proposal development
  • Must be a US Citizen
  • Able to multi-task in a deadline-driven environment
  • High level of customer service, attention to detail, and ability to meet tight deadlines
