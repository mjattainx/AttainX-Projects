We have extensive experience and expertise in providing InfoSec support to our customers. Our team employs technical capabilities in managing the processes, tools and policies to protect your organization’s sensitive and private information from threats such as malware and phishing attacks, identity theft and ransomware. Our approach to InfoSec follows the core objectives of Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability (CIA). The CIA approach includes the following:

  • Confidentiality: We ensure that your organization’s sensitive and critical information is not disclosed to any unauthorized individual and entity.
  • Integrity: We provide solutions that prevent unauthorized modification of data and maintaining its accuracy and completeness.
  • Availability: Our solution ensures that the data is available to authorized users, when requested.

Our organization specializes in various areas of InfoSec, which include network security, securing information systems and their databases, security testing, security audits, disaster recovery, and maintaining continuity of operations in the event of catastrophic failure. We implement various security controls to deter attackers and mitigate system vulnerabilities. Our security specialists will collaborate with you to successfully navigate the ATO process and develop POA&Ms to document and remediate security weaknesses in your systems.
